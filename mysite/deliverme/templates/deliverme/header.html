    • Tutorial

    This is our home page

    There is nothing to do right now because you need to sign in.

    Click the sign up button at the top right corner.

    It will bring you to this page where you can sign up.

    Once you signed up, it will bring you back to the home screen.

    Now click the Log in button to log in to your new account, this screen will show.

    Now that you logged in you can see that two buttons are added.

    One if you are making a request and another if you want to do deliver something.

    If you clicked one of the add buttons, this popup will show up.

    When you click make request, a new task will show up.

    If you click wallet on it will take you to a page where you can login to your venmo account.

    If you click chat log it will take you to a page for our chat feature.

    You are required to log in again for security reasons.

    Lastly, you can check your profile by clicking on your name and then clicking settings.

    All the pictures are the same because we want it to be anonymous.

    Also no one else can see your profile so don't worry about your information getting leaked.
